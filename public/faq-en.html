FAQ

Common questions about World Puzzle in its flag-focused version.

Do I need an account?

No. You can play without signing up.

Can I play on mobile?

Yes. Mobile gameplay is adapted: tap a flag to select it, then tap the matching country. Drag and drop is not required.

Which browsers are recommended?

Use an up-to-date browser like Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge.

Why do I see a cookie/ads prompt?

The site can display AdSense ads if you allow it. You can decline from the consent banner. Details are on the Privacy page.

Where is ads.txt?

It is available at /ads.txt.

Where does map data come from?

Natural Earth public domain data, adapted for in-browser gameplay.
