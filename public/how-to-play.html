How to play FlagRace

FlagRace now focuses on one mode: match each flag with the correct country.

1) Pick a map

On the home screen, select a region on the world map (Europe, Africa, Asia, North & Central America, South America), then press Start game.

2) Place flags

The flag dock is shown on the left on desktop and at the bottom on mobile. Your goal is to place every flag on its country.

Desktop

  • Drag a flag from the dock to the right country.
  • Use zoom for small countries.
  • Your score changes with progress and time.

Mobile / touch

  • Tap a flag to select it (one at a time).
  • Then tap the matching country on the map.
  • No drag and drop required on mobile.

3) Useful controls

  • Zoom: + / − / Reset buttons, or touch gestures depending on device.
  • Dock navigation: use ‹ › to browse flags in compact mode.
  • Give up: ends the game with score/time penalty.

4) End of game

When all flags are placed, you get a summary with time and score, then you can replay or return to menu.
