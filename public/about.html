About FlagRace

FlagRace is a browser game focused on one challenge: match flags to countries.

Project goal

Deliver a fast and accessible geography training game with no installation and no account required.

Map data

Map layers are based on Natural Earth public domain datasets, adapted for interactive rendering.

How it runs

The game runs in your browser. On mobile, gameplay is optimized for tap-to-place interactions.

Ads and privacy
